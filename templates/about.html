Pāṇḍiytatāraka, or Pāṇḍitya for short, provides a visual way to explore relationships between works and authors in the Pandit Prosopographical Database of Indic Texts. Pāṇḍitya is in conversation with Pandit, but it is not officially affiliated with it.
